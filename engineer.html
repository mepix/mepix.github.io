Tinker

From Lego to 3D Printing, I like to build things. Here are a few of the projects I have worked on.

Robotics Project (Tanner Research)

During the summer of 2015, I joined a robotics project at Tanner Research. By the end of the Summer, Dr. Tanner and I were ready to test our robot in the field!

FuturizeX Competition

This is my submission for the FuturizeX Competition, sponsored by UCLA and the X Prize, where I discuss the future of large format 3D Printing.

8-Bit JukeBox

I found this record player in a thrift store... and it need a lot of TLC. I decided to restore it and add my own modifications. I built a new grill front for it out of wood and attached a few LEDs behind the speaker cloth. I am still improving the code for the Arduino to analyze the incoming audio signal.

3D Printing

Previous Next

I have tinkered with this large format 3D Printer for 3 years as an independent project at Tanner Research. It has a 2 foot cubed print volume and is capable of printing with both PLA and ABS Plastic.

Electric Vehicle Chargers

At UCLA's Smart Grid Energy Research Center, I manufactured electric vehicle chargers. I started handling fabrication of various components and I ultimately wound up supervising a team of 10 undergraduates to manufacture the devices.
